Location

Northeastern’s West Village H, room 366

7 August 2017, starting at 9:30am

The building is located at the intersection of Huntington Ave and Parker/Forsythe. It is the tallest building in the neighborhood, diagonally across the street from the Museum of Fine Arts. To reach the elevator to the third floor, go into the inner building on the ground floor; if you encounter a front desk, you’re in the wrong part of the building. When you exit on the third floor, turn right and you have arrived.
